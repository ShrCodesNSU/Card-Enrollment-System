Welcome to
Dhaka Metro Rail.

From our website, you can:



Travel All Across Dhaka

Apply for our MetroCard so that you can gain access to the brand new Dhaka Metro Rail. This will allow you to Travel faster than ever before and without even having to stand in long queues for a ticket!

Search and Prebook

Afraid of loosing your train or not getting a seat? Well, throw that thought out of the window as you can always hop into the "Booking" tab to see what are your available options and how many seats are left!

Add Funds

You can use your debit/credit card to add funds to your card both manually through various banks or directly through our website online. This will save you the hassel of paying a visit to the bank!



How do we Work?



Instructions:

● Sign-up in our website

● Apply for your card

● Our officials will verify your details

● You will get a date for your bio verification

● Our officials will confirm your bio verification

● Your MetroCard will be manufactured and delivered

● You can now add funds to your account

● After that you can see the available trips

● Prebook your trips to ensure your seat

● Keep a track of your account
